JACK ZHANG MAGIC

ABOUT ME

I love magic

Jack mainly offers two kind of shows: stage and walk-around. Stage magic is meant to be for the special occasion where audiences gather together to watch a show. This type of magic is suitable for bigger crowds, where the tricks are visual and entertaining. Jack has performed for various stages and events, including a TED talk invitation, multiple Chinese New Year shows, university events, etc. On the other hand, walk-round magic is perfect for a cocktail party situation where guests are either standing or sitting at tables, and Jack moves around to perform for each individual groups. This type of magic is more interactive with audiences and makes a more personal relationship. Using simple props like cards, coins, borrowed objects he can thoroughly entertain the guests. In the past, he has performed for companies year-end parties such as Google, Facebook, Amazon, D2L, etc. He has also done other form of gigs including weddings, private parties, semi-formals, etc.

Jack Zhang


Junchen Zhang (Jack) was from Toronto, currently residing in San Francisco Bay Area and working at Google for full-time. He has years of experience in performing magic, specializing in card magic. He believes that card carries the same importance as piano in the music instruments world, as it could be utilized to perform any kind of harmonious miracles. He is also proficient at coins, fire, reading minds, and making things appear. Jack always put entertainment value first before anything else, he is a firm believer that the audiences have to be laughing and thoroughly entertained before they get amazed, thus it is always fun to work with Jack’s performing style. Jack got invested in magic at a young age, there are rumors saying that when he was born, the first thing he said to a nurse was: “Please pick a card!” His first encounter of magic was seeing David Copperfield on TV when the latter made The Statue of Liberty disappeared into thin air. That was a definitive moment for Jack to fall in love with the art of magic. He started to buy magic toys to perform to classmates in elementary school. In university he was the president of magic club, where he taught magic tricks to members and lead them to perform for various school events. He stated to offer professional service back in 2013, when he started to do paid gigs for parties and events.
